About SkillHub

The premier hyperlocal student skill marketplace.

Project Description

SkillHub is a student-to-student marketplace designed specifically for campus environments and local neighborhoods. It serves as a central hub where students can post their unique skills—from programming and graphic design to tutoring and resume building—and collaborate seamlessly. By focusing geographically, SkillHub ensures that help is always just around the corner.

Our Purpose

The primary purpose of the platform is to empower students by bridging the gap between talent and demand within academic communities. Often, students possess high-value skills but lack a secure, localized platform to offer them. Simultaneously, others struggle with assignments or personal projects and need peer-level assistance. We provide the ecosystem to facilitate these connections efficiently and securely.

Benefits for Students

Monetize Your Skills

Earn income doing what you're good at, natively integrated with our flexible payment system.

Affordable Peer Help

Get quality academic or creative assistance at student-friendly rates.

Build a Portfolio

Secure & Unified
